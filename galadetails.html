EVENING IN THE MARITIMES

Save the date June 5th, 2014

Hilton Lac Leamy

    Evening in the Maritimes is the best lobster dinner in the region and has been for the last 19 years!

  • Evening in the Maritimes features a 5-course lobster dinner; fresh North Atlantic lobster, seafood chowder, salads and desserts. East Coast entertainment creates the feel of an east coast kitchen party. A silent auction featuring extravagant jewellery, appliances, artwork, unique gift baskets, travel, golf, and other items raises funds for Citizen Advocacy. An exclusive balloon raffle, with all balloons popped in unison, sees guests win items such as trips, $1,000 cash, trips to Nova Scotia, and hot air balloon rides. We also feature a live auction with items such as cross continental trips and exclusive catered dinners with Ottawa celebrities. Honourary Co-Chairs Senator Vernon White and CTV Ottawa news anchor Graham Richardson will welcome guests, and the night’s MC will host the evening.
